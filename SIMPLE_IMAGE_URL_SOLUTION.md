## 🎨 **How It Works**

### **User Experience:**
1. **Click "Add Featured Image"** → URL input appears

3. **Click "Add Image"** → Tests if image loads
4. **Success!** → Image appears immediately in your app

- ✅ **File Extensions**: `.jpg`, `.png`, `.gif`, `.webp`, `.svg`
